# Process Management Framework

## Overview

This document outlines the **process management framework** for our organization. It covers key workflows, responsibilities, and approval chains.

## 1. Core Processes

### 1.1 Request Intake

All requests must be submitted through the official intake form. The following information is required:

- Requestor name and department
- Priority level (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
- Expected completion date
- Business justification

> **Note:** Requests without proper justification will be returned to the requestor.

### 1.2 Approval Workflow

| Stage                | Approver              | SLA             |
| -------------------- | --------------------- | --------------- |
| Initial Review       | Team Lead             | 2 business days |
| Technical Assessment | Subject Matter Expert | 5 business days |
| Final Approval       | Department Manager    | 3 business days |
